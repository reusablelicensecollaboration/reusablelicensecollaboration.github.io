Types of Licenses

Introduction

A license is a legally recognized grant of permission, which allows one party (the licensor) to grant rights to another party (the licensee). Licensing is the act of offering a license. Licenses are used by governments, companies, organizations, and individuals in a wide variety of ways. The type of license most people are familiar with is a driver's license, through which the government grants drivers permission to use public roads. There are many other types of licenses though such as Copyright licenses, Patent licenses, software licenses, and hunting licenses. Licenses can fall into more than one category too. For example, a software license might also be a form of Copyright license.

Creation, and Re-use
